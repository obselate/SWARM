About

Built for RMF Professionals

SWARM is a native desktop application designed for RMF professionals working within DoD and government environments. It provides a centralized interface for loading, reviewing, editing, and managing DISA STIG checklists. One interface replaces fragmented manual processes with a streamlined, high-performance workflow.

Every seat gets the full product. No feature gates, no tiers, no surprises. Load a directory, get to work.

High Performance

Loads and manages thousands of checklists with instant filtering and a responsive interface. Fast enough to keep up with your workflow.

Bulk Management

Load entire directories of CKL files, edit findings across thousands of checklists at once, and track unsaved changes at a glance.

Smart Filtering

Build custom filters to slice through findings by severity, status, STIG, or any combination. Export and share filters across your team.

Runs Anywhere

No installation. Standalone .EXE. No dependencies or internet access required. Drop it on any machine. Classified, unclassified, connected, or completely disconnected. It just works.

Capabilities

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

One tool for the entire STIG checklist workflow. From bulk loading to filtered review to final save.

Bulk Operations

Load, edit, save, and manage hundreds of checklists at once. Select a directory and SWARM handles the rest. Bulk status changes, bulk comments, bulk saves.

Advanced Filtering

Field-based search with operators. Filter by severity, status, STIG, hostname, or any combination. Filters apply instantly across 800K+ vulnerabilities.

Mitigations & Overlays

Apply answer files or overlays to auto-answer vulnerabilities in bulk. Preview exactly what will change before committing. Selectively apply only what you want.

Host Info Management

Manage hostname, IP, MAC, and FQDN across multiple checklists simultaneously. Update machine identity in bulk instead of one at a time.

STIG Version Updates

Update checklists to new STIG versions with intelligent matching. Findings, statuses, and comments carry over automatically. No manual re-entry.

Export & Reporting

Export host information to CSV or XLSX. Get data out in the format you need for reporting, audits, or inventory tracking.

Structured Search

Field-based queries with operators let you find exactly what you need across every loaded checklist. Instantly.

severity:medium status:open
OR
se:medium st:open
title:password,credential
OR
t:password,credential

OR
