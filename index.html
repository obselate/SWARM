About SWARM

SWARM is a native desktop application designed for RMF professionals working within DoD and government environments. It provides a centralized interface for loading, reviewing, editing, and managing DISA STIG checklists — replacing fragmented manual processes with a streamlined, high-performance workflow.


SWARM is fully standalone and portable — no installation, no cloud dependencies, no external services required. Drop it on a machine and it runs. Connected or disconnected, teams can be productive immediately without waiting on IT or infrastructure.


SWARM was designed by practitioners who live in the STIG ecosystem daily. Every feature exists because it solves a real problem - not because it looks good on a slide. The result is a tool that fits naturally into existing RMF workflows while dramatically reducing the time and effort required to maintain compliance posture.

Capabilities

Product Features

Everything you need to manage STIG compliance at scale

Bulk Operations
Load, edit, save, and manage hundreds of checklists at once. Select a directory and SWARM handles the rest — bulk status changes, bulk comments, bulk saves. Everything scales.
Advanced Filtering
Build powerful custom filters to slice through findings by severity, status, STIG, or any combination. Filters apply instantly across massive datasets — and can be exported and shared across your team.
Mitigations & Overlays
Apply answer files or overlays to auto-answer vulnerabilities in bulk. Preview exactly what will change before committing, and selectively apply only the findings you want.
Host Info Management
Manage hostname, IP, MAC, and FQDN across multiple checklists simultaneously. Update machine identity in bulk instead of editing checklists one at a time.
Export & Reporting
Export host information to CSV or XLSX. Get the data out in the format you need for reporting, audits, or inventory tracking.
Cross-Platform & Portable
Runs natively on Windows, macOS, and Linux. No installation, no dependencies, no network required — drop it on a machine and go.
